ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Pavan Vynatheya (pronounced vine-uh-tey-ya) and I am from Bangalore, India. I can speak English, Hindi, Kannada and Tamil. In 2020, I received my BS-MS Physics degree at Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Kolkata (IISER-K).

I am currently doing PhD soon at the Max Planck Institut für Astrophysik (MPA) at Garching, Germany. My research interest is computational stellar astrophysics.
